Chloe Boehm

I will be a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Alfred Wegener Institute for Polar and Marine Research starting in February 2025. My research broadly focuses on exploring large-scale climate variability, with a current focus on understanding the causes and consequences of recent Antarctic sea ice change. I am interested in understanding the linkages between the atmosphere, ocean, and sea ice, especially how these connections vary as a function of season and lag. I use a mixture of observationally-constrained data and models to parse out individual interactions within such a complex environment.
